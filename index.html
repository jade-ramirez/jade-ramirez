intentional spending challenge

At the Financial Wellness Center I designed and taught a course called Intentional Spending Challenge. The focus of the course was to inform students about their own spending and how to intentionally manage their spending. The course went over the psychology of spending, the history of consumerism, and current business practices that encourage spending.
